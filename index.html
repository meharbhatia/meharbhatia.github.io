Hello!

I am currently a full-time research assistant at Multimodal Digital Media Analysis Lab MIDAS Lab (IIIT-Delhi India), under the guidance of Dr. Rajiv Ratn Shah (IIIT-Delhi India).

My research interests include but are not limited to: Natural Langauge Undertanding, Spoken Language Processing, Multimodal Representation Learning, Adversarial Networks, Interpretability, Multimodal Fusion and co-learning, Studying algorithmic bias & fairness in NLP and Speech models, and data ethics in AI!

I have worked as a research intern at International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H) India for three consecutive summers during my undergraduation with Dr. Dipti Misra Sharma and Dr. Manish Shrivastava at Language Technologies Research Center (LTRC), and Dr. Y Raghu Reddy at Software Engineering Research Center (SERC).

In my free time, I enjoy cooking for my family and friends, exploring new places and whereabouts in my city, photography, making sketches, reading books, swimming and playing the Violin and Ukulele.

Feel free to send me a message about my work or just about anything else in the world. My pronouns are she/her/hers.
