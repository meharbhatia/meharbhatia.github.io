Calling Out Bluff

Under Review

Yaman Kumar, Mehar Bhatia, Anubha Kabra, Junyi Jessy Li, Di Jin, Rajiv Ratn Shah

We test out the natural language understanding capabilities and robustness of SOTA Automatic Essay scoring models using adversarial samples and discover what they actually score! With a majority of student's scores at stake, we provide an extensive adversarial suite that should be tested with each SOTA model before it is used for this purpose along with robust evaluation metrics to measure performance.
